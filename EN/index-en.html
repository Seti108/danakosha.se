Danakosha Sweden

Tibetan buddhism in the Nyingma tradition

Danakosha Sweden is a non-profit organization under the guidance of Tulku Dakpa Rinpoche. Our purpose is making buddhism more available in Sweden and the world in general. We host yearly courses in buddhism with Tulku Dakpa Rinpoche. Fell free to follow our newsletter for updates on our upcoming courses!

Tulku Dakpa Rinpoche

... born in 1975 as a son of Venerable Lama Thubten Kalsang and Yongdu Wangmo, and he was recognized by H.H. Mindrolling Trichen Rinpoche as the 3rd reincarnation of Drupwang Rogza Sonam Palge Rinpoche. He studied in the college of Mindrolling Monastery, and graduated as a certificated sutric and tantric holder of teachings. Currently he is the spiritual director of the Danakosha Dharma community. His great kindness and generosity of teachings is truly appreciated by his students.
