Danakosha Ling

In the summer of 2016 Danakosha Finland bought a retreat -and teaching center in Jokioinen, south-western Finland. Visit the link below for information about the activities at the center as well as Tulku Dakpa Rinpoche's international travel schedule.

To get to the center without a car, go by bus from Turku or Helsinki, to Forssa. From there one can take a bus/taxi to Jokioinen. Contakt the center for advice on your visit.
